Delivery & Turnaround

Yes We deliver anywhere

Order products online at Printarabia.ae, and have them delivered to your door. Or pick them up at our JLT office during business hours. Find out everything you need to know about delivery, product turnarounds, what they are and how long they take.

Can I collect my order?

Absolutely! You are welcome to collect your order in person from our JLT office.
In-office pick up service is limited to business hours only: 9am-6pm, Saturdays to Thursdays. Please call before hand to confirm your order is indeed ready and available for pick up to avoid any kind of disappointment.
Free parking can be avail at the parking area of the podium of Cluster C.
ATM machine is located next to Carrefour on the podium level. Open 24/7.
We strongly advise against in-office pickup when ordering products that are heavy or large in quantity.

What is Production Turnaround?

It is the estimated amount of time we need to complete a print job. This includes compiling the print data, printing, trimming, binding and any post-print finishings as well as packaging and preparing your order for delivery.

When does the turnaround time start?

Production starts only once you have confirmed the digital proof. If your uploaded data meets our printing requirements, we will send a link by email for you to confirm a digital proof of your data. After you approve the proof, your order will be sent to the factory for production.

How long does it take?

It depend on following these 3 factors:

Amount of time necessary for you to review and approve your digital proof.
Number of days we need for printing, plus any finishings.
Time required for your order to arrive at its destination.
